About Land of the Free Pub

Welcome to your new home away from home located in close proximity to uptown Charlotte, NC! Featuring a rustic atmosphere and several American favorites, along with newly released options, you are bound to find something you will love here. We are a family owned business and have been open for the past twenty years, serving guests who have quickly become like family to us. We hope to meet you soon at our neighborhood pub, where freedom takes flight!
